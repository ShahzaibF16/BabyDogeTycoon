Why invest in BabyDogeTycoon?

The buy-back wallet consists of BNB that is taken from the tax on each buy / sell transaction. When an individual sells, they add tokens to the liquidity pool while taking BNB. The buy-back wallet automatically counter-buys the sell, injecting BNB back into the pool while simultaneously burning the tokens that are bought again.
